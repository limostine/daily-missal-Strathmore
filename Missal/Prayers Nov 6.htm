NOVEMBER 6 All Saints of Africa

ENTRANCE ANTIPHON

The righteous will flourish like the palm tree, and grow like a Lebanon cedar. Planted in the house of the Lord, they will flourish in the courts of our God.

Introductory Rites

COLLECT

God, our Father, who raised up in the Church of Africa many men and women outstanding in holiness; grant us, we beseech you, the gift of your grace, that we may follow their example in order to be, one day, their fellow citizens in heaven. Through our Lord Jesus Christ, your Son, who lives and reigns with you in the unity of the Holy Spirit, one God, for ever and ever.

Readings

PRAYER OVER THE OFFERINGS

Father of love and mercy, receive our prayers and gifts on this feast of the Saints of Africa; may this celebration bring us closer to you, the source of life and holiness, and give us renewed vigour in living our baptismal call. Through Christ our Lord.

Preface of All Saints

COMMUNION ANTIPHON

Come, you whom my Father has blessed. Inherit the kingdom prepared for you from the foundation of the world.

PRAYER AFTER COMMUNION

Father of life, who have nourished us at the table of the Word and of the Eucharist; strengthened by these gifts, and sustained by the example and prayer of the Saints of Africa, may we work for the spreading of your Kingdom in holiness and love. Through Christ our Lord.
