WEDNESDAY BEFORE EPIPHANY

ENTRANCE ANTIPHON

The people who walked in darkness have seen a great light; on those who lived in the shadow of death, light has shone.

Introductory Rites

OPENING PRAYER

All-powerful Father, you sent your Son Jesus Christ to bring the new light of salvation to the world. May he enlighten us with his radiance, who lives and reigns...

Readings

PRAYER OVER THE GIFTS

Father of peace, accept our devotion and sincerity, and by our sharing in this mystery draw us closer to each other and to you.

Preface of Christmas I-III

COMMUNION ANTIPHON

The eternal life which was with the Father has been revealed to us.

PRAYER AFTER COMMUNION

Lord, may this sacrament be our strength. Teach us to value all the good you give us and help us to strive for eternal life.
