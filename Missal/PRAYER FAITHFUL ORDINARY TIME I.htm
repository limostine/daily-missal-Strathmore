ORDINARY TIME I

 

Celebrant

Dear brothers and sisters, gathered as one to celebrate the good things we have received from our God, let us ask him to prompt in us prayers that are worthy of his hearing.

Intentions

1  V. For N. our Pope and N. our Bishop and all the clergy, with the people entrusted to their charge, let us pray to the Lord.

2  V. For those who hold public office and those who assist them in promoting the common good, let us pray to the Lord.

3  V. For those who travel by sea, land or air, for captives and all held held in prison, let us pray to the Lord.

4  V. For all of us gathered in this sacred place by faith and devotion and by love and reverence for God, let us pray to the Lord.

Celebrant

May the petitions of your Church be pleasing in your sight, O Lord, so that we may receive from your mercy what we cannot ask out of confidence in our own merits. Through Christ our Lord.

    R. Amen.
