FOR THE NATION OR STATE

Introductory Rites

COLLECT

O God, who arrange all things according to a wonderful design, graciously receive the prayers we pour out to you for our country (state), that, through the wisdom of its leaders and the integrity of its citizens, harmony and justice may be assured and lasting prosperity come with peace. Through our Lord Jesus Christ, your Son, who lives and reigns with you in the unity of the Holy Spirit, one God, for ever and ever.
