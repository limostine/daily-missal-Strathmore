JUNE 2 Saints Marcellinus and Peter, Martyrs

(+ 304) Marcellinus and Peter were martyred under Diocletian. Their names are included in the Roman Canon.

Common of Martyrs Easter or Outside Easter

Introductory Rites

COLLECT

O God, who surround us with protection through the glorious confession of the Martyrs Saints Marcellinus and Peter, grant that we may profit by imitating them and be upheld by their prayers. Through our Lord Jesus Christ, your Son, who lives and reigns with you in the unity of the Holy Spirit, one God, for ever and ever.

Readings Easter or Ordinary
