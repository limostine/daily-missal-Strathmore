OCTOBER 14 St Callistus I, Pope and Martyr

(+ 222). He provided for the burial of martyrs in the catacombs. Known for his mercy and understanding of sinners who repented from their sins, against the opinion of the Rigorists, mainly Tertullian. He fixed the discipline of the Sacrament of Penance. He defended the faith against the Adoptionist and Modalist heresies with regard to the Holy Trinity and the Person of Jesus Christ. He died a martyr in Rome during the reign of Alexander Severus.

Common of Martyrs or of Pastors (for popes)

Introductory Rites

COLLECT

O God, who raised up Pope Saint Callistus the First to serve the Church and attend devoutly to Christ’s faithful departed, strengthen us, we pray, by his witness to the faith, so that, rescued from the slavery of corruption, we may merit an incorruptible inheritance. Through our Lord Jesus Christ, your Son, who lives and reigns with you in the unity of the Holy Spirit, one God, for ever and ever.
