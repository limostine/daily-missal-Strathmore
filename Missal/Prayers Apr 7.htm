APRIL 7 St John Baptist de la Salle, Priest

(1651-1719). Born in Rheims, France. He was known as the Father of Modern Pedagogy. He opened free schools for poor children, introducing new teaching methods. He organised the congregation called the Brothers of the Christian Schools, which made great contributions to popular education.

Common of Pastors or of Holy Men & Women (for Educators)

Introductory Rites

COLLECT

O God, who chose Saint John Baptist de la Salle to educate young Christians, raise up, we pray, teachers in your Church ready to devote themselves wholeheartedly to the human and Christian formation of the young. Through our Lord Jesus Christ, your Son, who lives and reigns with you in the unity of the Holy Spirit, one God, for ever and ever.
