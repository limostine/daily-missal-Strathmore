MASSES FOR THE DEAD

 

Celebrant

Let us in faith call upon God the almighty Father, who raised Christ his Son from the dead, as we pray for the salvation of the living and the dead.

Intentions

1    V. That God may establish the Christian people in faith and unity, let us pray to the Lord.

2    V. That he may rescue the entire world from all the evils of war, let us pray to the Lord.

3    V. That he may be pleased to show himself a father to our brothers and sisters who lack work, food or housing, let us pray to the Lord.

4a  V. That he may be pleased to admit for ever to the company of the Saints his deceased servant N., who once through Baptism received the seed of eternal life, let us pray to the Lord.

4b  V. That on the last day he may raise up N., who fed on the Body of Christ, the Bread of eternal life, let us pray to the Lord.

           (or for a priest):

      V. That he may grant N. a share in the heavenly liturgy, for he exercised the priestly office in the Church, let us pray to the Lord.

4c   V. That he may grant to the souls of our brothers and sisters, friends and benefactors the reward of their labours, let us pray to the Lord.

4d  V. That he may welcome into the light of his face all who have fallen asleep in the hope of the resurrection, let us pray to the Lord.

4e   V. That he may graciously help and comfort our brothers and sisters who are suffering afliction, let us pray to the Lord.

4f   V. That he may be pleased to gather into his glorious Kingdom all who have gathered here in faith and devotion, let us pray to the Lord.

Celebrant

May the prayer of those who cry to you benefit the souls of your servants, O Lord: free them, we pray, from all their sins and make them sharers in your redemption. Through Christ our Lord.

    R. Amen.
