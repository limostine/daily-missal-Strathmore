DECEMBER 1 Bl. Clementine Anuarite, Virgin & Martyr

A Roman Catholic nun who chose to be killed rather than surrender her virginity in Zaire's civil war in 1964.

Common of Martyrs or Common of Virgins

COLLECT

O God our Father, who have called Blessed Anuarite to follow the Lord through a life of holiness, faithfulness and martyrdom; grant, we beseech you, that we may follow Christ as she did and one day join her in your Kingdom. Through our Lord Jesus Christ, your Son, who lives and reigns with you in the unity of the Holy Spirit, one God, for ever and ever.
