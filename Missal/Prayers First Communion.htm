FIRST COMMUNION

The Mass of the solemnity of the Body and Blood of Christ or the Votive Mass of the Holy Eucharist may be celebrated on any day except the Sundays of Advent, Lent and Easter, the octave of Easter, the Commemoration of All the Faithful Departed, Ash Wednesday and the weekdays of Holy Week.

Readings from the Mass of the day (in full or in part) or Special Readings.
