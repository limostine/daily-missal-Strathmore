MAY 29 Blessed Joseph Gerard, Priest

In Botswana, South Africa and Swaziland

Common of Pastors: For One Pastor

Introductory Rites

COLLECT

God, source of peace and giver of rain and abundance, who called the Priest Blessed Joseph Gerard, to bring the light of the Gospel to the peoples of southern Africa; grant that through his intercession we may share his love for the poor and work for peace and reconciliation in the world. Through our Lord Jesus Christ, your Son, who lives and reigns with you in the unity of the Holy Spirit, one God, for ever and ever.
