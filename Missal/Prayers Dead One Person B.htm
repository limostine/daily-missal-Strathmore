FOR ONE PERSON 2

ENTRANCE ANTIPHON

I know that my Redeemer lives, and that he will at last stand forth upon the dust; and form my flesh I shall see God.

Introductory Rites

OPENING PRAYER

Free your servant N., we pray, O Lord, from every bond of sin, that he (she), who in this world was found worthy to be conformed to Christ, may be raised to the glory of the resurrection and draw the breath of new life among your Saints. Through our Lord Jesus Christ, your Son, who lives and reigns with you in the unity of the Holy Spirit, one God, for ever and ever.

Readings Proper or Others

PRAYER OVER THE GIFTS

Grant our supplications, we pray, O Lord, that this sacrifice may benefit your departed servant N., since through its offering you have loosed the offences of all the world. Through Christ our Lord.

PREFACE of Christian Death I-V

COMMUNION ANTIPHON

This is the bread that comes down from heaven, says the Lord; so that one may eat it and not die.

PRAYER AFTER COMMUNION

May the sacrifice of your Church, we pray, O Lord, benefit the soul of your servant N., so that he (she), who received the Sacrament of Christ’s mercy, may enter his company, together with your Saints. Through Christ our Lord.
