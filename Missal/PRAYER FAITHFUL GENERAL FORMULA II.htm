GENERAL FORMULA II

 

Celebrant

Brothers and sisters, as we now make our prayer for our community and for the world, let us all pray to Christ the Lord, not only for ourselves and our own needs, but for the entire people.

Intentions

1a  V. For the whole Christian people, let us beseech the abundance of divine goodness.

       R. Christ hear us. or Christ, graciously hear us.

1b  V. For all who do not yet believe, let us implore the giver of all spiritual gifts.

       R. Christ hear us.

2a  V. For For those who hold public office, let us call upon the power of the Lord.

       R. Christ hear us.

2b  V. For favorable weather and abundant fruits from the earth, let us entreat the Lord, the ruler of the world.

       R. Christ hear us.

3a  V. For our brothers and sisters who cannot be present at this sacred assembly, let us beseech him who observes all things.

       R. Christ hear us.

3b  V. For the repose of the souls of the faithful departed, let us call upon the judge of all humanity.

       R. Christ hear us.

4a  V. For all of us who pray in faith and ask the mercy of the Lord, let us entreat the compassion of our Saviour.

       R. Christ hear us.

4b  V. For ourselves and those close to us who await the Lord’s goodness, let us call upon the mercy of Christ the Lord.

       R. Christ hear us.

Celebrant

Incline your merciful ear to our prayers, we ask, O Lord, and listen in kindness to the supplications of those who call on you. Through Christ our Lord.

    R. Amen.
