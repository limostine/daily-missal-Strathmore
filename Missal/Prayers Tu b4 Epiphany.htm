TUESDAY BEFORE EPIPHANY

ENTRANCE ANTIPHON

Blessed is he who comes in the name of the Lord; the Lord God shines upon us.

Introductory Rites

OPENING PRAYER

God our Father, when your Son was born of the Virgin Mary he became like us in all things but sin. May we who have been reborn in him be free from our sinful ways. We ask this through our Lord Jesus Christ, your Son, who lives and reigns with you and the Holy Spirit, one God, for ever and ever.

Readings

PRAYER OVER THE GIFTS

Father, in your mercy accept our gifts. By sharing in this eucharist may we come to live more fully the love we profess. Grant this through Christ our Lord.

Preface of Christmas I-III

COMMUNION ANTIPHON

God loved us so much that he sent his own Son in the likeness of sinful flesh.

PRAYER AFTER COMMUNION

God our Father, in this eucharist you touch our lives. Keep your love alive in our hearts that we may become worthy of you. We ask this through Christ our Lord.
