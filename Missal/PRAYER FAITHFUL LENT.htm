LENT

 

Celebrant

We should pour forth prayers at all times, dear brothers and sisters, but, above all, in these days of Lent we ought to watch more intently with Christ and direct our petitions more fervently to God.

Intentions

1  V. For the whole Christian people, that in this sacred time they may be more abundantly nourished by every word that comes from the mouth of God, let us pray to the Lord.

2  V. For the whole world, that in lasting tranquillity and peace our days may truly become the acceptable time of grace and salvation, let us pray to the Lord.

3  V. For sinners and the neglectful, that in this time of reconciliation they may return to Christ, let us pray to the Lord.

4  V. For ourselves, that God may at last stir up in our hearts aversion for our sins, let us pray to the Lord.

Celebrant

Grant, we pray, O Lord, that your people may turn to you with all their heart, so that whatever they dare to ask in fitting prayer they may receive by your mercy. Through Christ our Lord.

    R. Amen.
